About the Indigenous Voices Archive

Preserving linguistic diversity and supporting community-led language revitalization efforts

Indigenous languages represent thousands of years of cultural knowledge and heritage. Many face endangerment today. This platform connects Indigenous communities, linguists, and language learners with resources for language preservation and revitalization.

Find resources for learners, documentation projects led by native speakers, and tools to help communities preserve ancestral languages. This space supports those reconnecting with heritage languages, participating in revitalization efforts, or learning about Indigenous cultures.
